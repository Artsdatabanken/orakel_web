Artsorakel (version [Version number])

Artsdatabanken's Artsorakel attempts to identify species in images using artificial intelligence. Without an account and completely free.

When the app is used, the model suggests what the image most closely resembles from the species it has been trained with. This means it can only suggest species that exist in nature and have been reported with images. It therefore only recognizes wild species (no humans, domestic animals, garden plants, etc.) and provides answers at species level (and some subspecies).

The app is continuously improved using images from Artsobservasjoner.no, and the recognition model is developed in collaboration with Naturalis Biodiversity Center. Images are sent to the server for recognition. Images and user information are not made available to Artsdatabanken or others.

Questions and feedback can be sent to support@artsobservasjoner.no.
